Privacy Policy

Welcome to AstroSnap! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Astraverse collects some personal information from you when you register on the app. This includes

  • name
  • username
  • email address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to

  • create and manage your account,
  • store and display your posts and images,
  • help you connect and communicate with other users,
  • monitor and improve app usage through Google Analytics,
  • enhance app functionality and fix issues with Crashlytics reporting.

3. Third-Party Services

Astraverse utilizes Firebase services as backend. These services may collect and process users' information like name, username, email, and other user-generated data as governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these services to understand how their data is handled. Third party services used are

  • Firebase for authentication, database, and storage,
  • Google Analytics for app analytics information,
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting.

By using Astraverse, you agree to our privacy policy.
