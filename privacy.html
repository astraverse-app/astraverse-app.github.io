Privacy Policy

Welcome to Astraverse! Your privacy is important to us. Here’s how we handle your information:

1. Information We Collect

Astraverse collects personal information from users when they register on the app. This includes usernames (which is created by the user while registering), name, and email address. Additionally, user-generated content such as astrophotography images and related data may be stored and shared within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected by Astraverse is used to provide services. User-generated content, including astrophotography images and comments, are publicly visible on Astraverse.

3. Third-Party Services

Astraverse utilizes third-party services, such as Firebase by Google, for backend infrastructure and analytics. These services may collect and process users' information like name, username, email, and other user-generated data as governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these services to understand how their data is handled.

4. Security

Almost all user data, including usernames, email addresses, profile information, and user-generated content such as astrophotography images and comments, is transmitted to and stored on Firebase servers.

By using Astraverse, you agree to our privacy policy.
